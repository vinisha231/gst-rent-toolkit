# How to use the GST Rent Toolkit

1. **Owners tab** — add each person/business that owns property, with their name
   and 15-character GSTIN. Each owner files their own returns.
2. **Units / Tenants tab** — add one row per rented unit: choose the owner, the
   tenant, the monthly rent, the GST rate (18% for commercial rent) and the
   **place of supply** (the state where the property is).
3. **Dashboard tab** — pick the filing month. You'll see:
   - the total taxable rent, CGST, SGST/IGST and total GST for the month, and
   - a card per owner with each invoice and the **GSTR-3B 3.1(a)** figures to file.
4. **Print / Save PDF** — keep a monthly record or share with your accountant.
5. **Data tab** — export a backup file, import it on another device, load sample
   data, or clear everything.

> Tip: the rent you enter is the **taxable value**. GST is added on top, so the
> tenant pays `rent + GST`.
